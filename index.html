About me

Hello World! I'm Ayanna, and I’m a future Full-Stack Developer. I'm a consummate problem-solver - I've always been fascinated with the way things work, the individual steps behind complex processes, the 'hows' of situations and the 'hows' of the potential remedies. I love solving puzzles - riddles, sudoku, simultaneous equations, whodunits, jigsaws, you name it!

My passion for logic questions and analysis has taken me on a winding path professionally – from a brief stint at the United Nations, to 8 years at the National Domestic Violence Hotline in my home of Trinidad and Tobago, as an Active Listener, a Team Leader and eventually the Manager. Today, I work as an HRIS Analyst, where I forage through and manage lots of data, hunting down answers to myriad questions from all levels of the organization.

I believe that these experiences have all shaped my new goal of becoming a developer, and led me here to the UCF Full-Stack Coding Boot Camp! I’m looking forward to a whole new level of problem-solving and to broadening my sleuthing skill-set.
